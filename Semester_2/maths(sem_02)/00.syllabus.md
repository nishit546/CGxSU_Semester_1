# Syllabus

## Unit 1: Differential & Multivariable Calculus

### Topics:

* Limits and continuity of functions
* Derivatives and rules of differentiation
* Taylor and Maclaurin series (basic expansions)
* Functions of two variables
* Partial derivatives and chain rule
* Gradient (basic computation and interpretation)
* Maxima and minima:

  * Single-variable functions
  * Two-variable functions
  * First and second derivative tests
* Applications:

  * Optimization problems
  * Basic machine learning loss functions

---

## Unit 2: Differential Equations

### Topics:

* First-order differential equations:

  * Variable separable form
  * Linear differential equations
* Second-order linear differential equations:

  * Constant coefficients (homogeneous cases)
* Laplace Transform:

  * Definition
  * Basic properties
  * Solving simple differential equations
* Applications:

  * Dynamic systems
  * Computational modeling

---

## Unit 3: Linear Algebra (Advanced Concepts)

### Topics:

* Vector spaces and subspaces
* Linear independence
* Basis and dimension
* Orthogonality and projections
* Eigenvalues and eigenvectors (revision + advanced problems)
* Diagonalization of matrices (basic problems)
* Positive definite matrices (basic concepts)
* Applications:

  * Data representation
  * Dimensionality reduction

---

## Unit 4: Numerical Methods & Optimization

### Topics:

* Root-finding methods:

  * Bisection method
  * Newton–Raphson method
* Solving linear systems:

  * Gauss elimination method
* Gradient Descent:

  * Basic concept
  * Simple numerical illustration
* Applications:

  * Optimization problems
  * Machine learning training algorithms
