# **LeetCode Practice & DSA Assignment**

## **1. Objective**

Strengthen problem-solving and DSA skills using:

* LeetCode Log Sheet: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HmiBvclG6p337qkLtrbneWt4LHpcQ5mobKUFEtV_fBM/edit?usp=sharing

* Striver A2Z DSA Sheet: https://takeuforward.org/dsa/strivers-a2z-sheet-learn-dsa-a-to-z

---

## **2. Problem Solving Requirement**

* Minimum **160 questions must be solved on LeetCode**
* Questions must be taken from:

  * Shared Sheet
  * Striver A2Z Sheet

---

## **3. YouTube Requirement**

* Minimum **40 videos must be uploaded**

### **Video Rules**

* Explanation must be in **English**
* Student’s **face must be clearly visible** in the video
* Explain problem → approach → code
* Code must be written during explanation
* Title = exact LeetCode question title

### **Description Must Include**

* Approach
* GitHub link
* LeetCode profile
* LinkedIn
* Any notes

## **4. Programming Requirement**

* Language: **C++ only**
* Must use **STL wherever applicable**

---

## **5. Weekly Targets**

* Duration: ~8 weeks

### **Per Week Minimum**

* **20 questions per week**
* **5 videos per week**

---

## **6. Viva Process**

* **Bi-weekly 1:1 viva sessions**
* Total **4 vivas before 20th June**
* Students must be able to explain and solve questions live

---

## **7. Deadline**

* **15th June (Final Submission)**
* Requirements by deadline:

  * **160+ questions solved**
  * **40 videos completed**

---

## **8. Scoring (Total: 60 Marks)**

* **Problem Solving (10 Marks)**

  * Total questions solved
  * Difficulty level covered

* **Viva Performance (20 Marks)**

  * Understanding
  * Ability to solve live

* **YouTube Videos (20 Marks)**

  * Clarity of explanation
  * Proper structure

* **Consistency (10 Marks)**

  * Weekly progress
  * Regular practice

---

## **9. Rules & Guidelines**

* No copying without understanding

* Must be able to explain any solved question

* Use proper STL and optimized approach

* Maintain consistency

* Videos must be original

* No fake progress or duplicate content
