# **Unit 2 – MCQ Questions**

## **Ecosystems and Biodiversity**

---

## **Section A: Basic Concepts (1–15)**

1. An ecosystem is:
   a) Only living organisms
   b) Only non-living components
   c) Interaction between living and non-living components
   d) Only plants
   **Answer: c**

---

2. Producers are:
   a) Animals
   b) Plants
   c) Humans
   d) Decomposers
   **Answer: b**

---

3. Consumers are:
   a) Autotrophs
   b) Producers
   c) Heterotrophs
   d) Decomposers
   **Answer: c**

---

4. Decomposers include:
   a) Plants
   b) Animals
   c) Bacteria and fungi
   d) Fish
   **Answer: c**

---

5. Which is an abiotic component?
   a) Grass
   b) Soil
   c) Deer
   d) Tiger
   **Answer: b**

---

6. The primary source of energy in an ecosystem is:
   a) Water
   b) Soil
   c) Sun
   d) Air
   **Answer: c**

---

7. Trophic level refers to:
   a) Climate
   b) Feeding level
   c) Soil type
   d) Temperature
   **Answer: b**

---

8. First trophic level consists of:
   a) Carnivores
   b) Herbivores
   c) Producers
   d) Decomposers
   **Answer: c**

---

9. Herbivores are:
   a) Primary consumers
   b) Secondary consumers
   c) Producers
   d) Decomposers
   **Answer: a**

---

10. Carnivores are:
    a) Producers
    b) Herbivores
    c) Secondary consumers
    d) Decomposers
    **Answer: c**

---

11. A food chain shows:
    a) Water cycle
    b) Energy flow
    c) Air movement
    d) Soil formation
    **Answer: b**

---

12. Food web is:
    a) Single chain
    b) Multiple interconnected chains
    c) Only producers
    d) Only consumers
    **Answer: b**

---

13. Biodiversity refers to:
    a) Only plants
    b) Only animals
    c) Variety of life
    d) Only microorganisms
    **Answer: c**

---

14. Genetic diversity means:
    a) Different ecosystems
    b) Variation within species
    c) Different species
    d) Only animals
    **Answer: b**

---

15. Ecosystem diversity refers to:
    a) Variety of genes
    b) Variety of species
    c) Variety of ecosystems
    d) Variety of plants
    **Answer: c**
