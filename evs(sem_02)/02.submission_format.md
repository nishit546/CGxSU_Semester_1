# **Environmental Science (BECE0201) – Case Study Presentation Assignment**

---

## **Submission Date**

**21st June**

---

## **Assignment Overview**

Students are required to create **one case study** based on their understanding of any topic/unit covered in Environmental Science (EVS).

Students may refer to the **14 case study examples provided* for guidance and inspiration. However, the submitted case study should be developed independently and should demonstrate the student's own understanding, analysis, and learning.

---

## **Case Study Requirements**

Select any topic from the EVS syllabus, such as:

* Ecosystem Imbalance
* Biodiversity Conservation
* Natural Resource Depletion
* Environmental Pollution
* Waste Management
* Climate Change
* Sustainable Development
* Any other topic covered in the EVS curriculum

The case study should include:

* Introduction
* Background of the issue
* Problem identification
* Causes and contributing factors
* Environmental and societal impacts
* Practical solutions and recommendations
* Key learnings and understanding gained from the study
* Conclusion

---

## **Presentation (PPT) Requirements**

Students must prepare a PowerPoint Presentation containing:

* **8 to 12 slides**
* Clear and well-structured content
* Relevant images, diagrams, charts, or visual representations
* Concise explanations (avoid text-heavy slides)
* Proper formatting and readability

Suggested Structure:

1. Title Slide
2. Introduction
3. Background
4. Problem Description
5. Causes
6. Impacts
7. Analysis
8. Solutions
9. Learning Outcomes
10. Conclusion
11. References (if applicable)
12. Thank You

---

## **Submission Requirements**

Students must submit:

1. **GR Number**
2. **PPT File**
3. **Video Explanation Link** (recorded presentation explaining the PPT)
4. **Google Drive Public Link** containing all required files

### Important

* Ensure that the Google Drive link is publicly accessible.
* The PPT and video must be accessible through the submitted link.
* Students are responsible for verifying access permissions before submission.

---

## **Evaluation Criteria**

* Understanding of the selected topic
* Quality of analysis
* Practicality of proposed solutions
* Demonstration of learning outcomes
