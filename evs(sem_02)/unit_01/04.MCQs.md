# **Unit 1 – MCQ Questions**

## **Environmental Science**

---

## **Section A: Basic Concepts (1–15)**

1. Environmental Science deals with:
   a) Only plants
   b) Only animals
   c) Interaction between living and non-living components
   d) Only pollution
   **Answer: c**

---

2. Which is a biotic component?
   a) Air
   b) Soil
   c) Plant
   d) Water
   **Answer: c**

---

3. Which is an abiotic component?
   a) Bacteria
   b) Fungi
   c) Water
   d) Animals
   **Answer: c**

---

4. The largest component of atmosphere is:
   a) Oxygen
   b) Nitrogen
   c) Carbon dioxide
   d) Hydrogen
   **Answer: b**

---

5. Hydrosphere includes:
   a) Land
   b) Air
   c) Water
   d) Plants
   **Answer: c**

---

6. Lithosphere refers to:
   a) Water
   b) Air
   c) Land
   d) Life
   **Answer: c**

---

7. Biosphere includes:
   a) Only plants
   b) Only animals
   c) All living organisms
   d) Only microorganisms
   **Answer: c**

---

8. Environmental Science is:
   a) Single subject
   b) Multidisciplinary subject
   c) Only biology
   d) Only chemistry
   **Answer: b**

---

9. Which of the following is not a natural component?
   a) Air
   b) Water
   c) Plastic
   d) Soil
   **Answer: c**

---

10. Which gas is essential for breathing?
    a) Nitrogen
    b) Oxygen
    c) Carbon dioxide
    d) Helium
    **Answer: b**

---

11. Which sphere supports life?
    a) Atmosphere
    b) Lithosphere
    c) Biosphere
    d) Hydrosphere
    **Answer: c**

---

12. Soil belongs to:
    a) Hydrosphere
    b) Atmosphere
    c) Lithosphere
    d) Biosphere
    **Answer: c**

---

13. Sunlight is an example of:
    a) Biotic component
    b) Abiotic component
    c) Living component
    d) Organic component
    **Answer: b**

---

14. The environment consists of:
    a) Only living things
    b) Only non-living things
    c) Both living and non-living
    d) Only water
    **Answer: c**

---

15. Microorganisms are:
    a) Abiotic
    b) Biotic
    c) Non-living
    d) Chemical
    **Answer: b**
