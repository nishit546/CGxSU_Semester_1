# **Environmental Science (BECE0201) – Presentation Based Examination**

---

## **Exam Schedule**

* **Dates:** 13th June to 16th June
* **Mode:** Presentation + Viva

---

## **Exam Format**

Each student will:

1. Prepare a **case study-based presentation**
2. Present their work in class
3. Appear for a **viva (oral examination)** after the presentation

---

## **Topic Selection**

Students must select **any ONE case study topic** from the following areas:

* Ecosystem imbalance
* Biodiversity loss or conservation
* Natural resource depletion (water, forest, minerals, energy)
* Environmental pollution (air, water, soil, noise)

Students are expected to:

* Clearly explain the **problem**
* Analyze the **causes and impacts**
* Propose **practical and realistic solutions**

---

## **Submission Requirements**

### **1. Presentation (PPT)**

Students must prepare a PowerPoint presentation with the following:

* **8 to 12 slides**
* Clear structure covering:

  * Introduction
  * Background
  * Environmental issue
  * Impacts
  * Solutions
  * Conclusion

#### **Important Requirements for PPT**

* Include **relevant images, diagrams, or visualizations**
* Visuals must **support the explanation of the problem and proposed solutions**
* Avoid text-heavy slides; keep content clear and concise
* Maintain proper formatting and readability

---

## **Evaluation Criteria**

Students will be evaluated based on:

* Understanding of the topic
* Depth of analysis
* Clarity of explanation
* Quality and practicality of solutions
* Presentation skills
* Performance in viva

---

## **Important Guidelines**

* Choose a **real-world case study**
* Ensure content is **structured and relevant**
* Maintain originality (avoid copying)
* Focus on **problem-solving and practical solutions**
* Be prepared to answer questions during viva
